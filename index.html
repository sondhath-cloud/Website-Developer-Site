About Sondra Hathaway

My career spans both public and private sectors, where I've always focused on helping organizations build positive habits and sustainable change. With an MBA, degrees in psychology and sociology, Prosci change management certification, and certification by the American Society for Quality as a Manager of Quality and Organizational Excellence, I bring a powerful mix of perspective to my work.

For decades I have painted with oils, but I believe my creativity is best expressed through code. Technology gives me an unconventional canvas to create beauty and nudge behavior and strengthen organizations. My technical skills are not just tools; they're a creative medium I use to design digital solutions that guide people toward better performance and lasting improvement.
